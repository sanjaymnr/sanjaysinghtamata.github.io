My Story

You know the tech. I speak the language.

I’ve worked across government, startups, and academia—designing systems, building products, and keeping critical data safe.

I started in engineering roles where “security” meant locked server rooms and last-minute audits. Over time, I realised the most secure organisations weren’t just using better tools—they were asking better questions.

Since then, I’ve focused on connecting strategy, security, and software delivery: aligning teams, simplifying complex architectures, and translating risk into clear trade-offs that leaders can act on.

What I believe Security isn’t about saying “no.” It’s about designing systems that make the safe path the easiest path—for users, engineers, and the business.
